Beans On Toast

Description

Beans on toast is a traditional British dish. Its simplicity is its strength -- it's easy to make, and absolutely delicious.

Keep reading to find out how to make the perfect plate of beans on toast!

Ingredients

  • 1 tin of baked beans
  • Two slices of white or brown bread
  • 50g butter

Steps

  1. Heat a full tin of beans gently in a pan for 3 minutes, stirring often.
  2. Pop your two slices of bread in the toaster.
  3. Butter your toast to your taste.
  4. Once cooked for 3 minutes, pour the beans out of the pan and onto the toast. Voila!
